  • Machine Learning Algorithm: We periodically generate and display content using our machine learning algorithm. This algorithm may collect and use data to improve the quality of the generated content.
  • Traffic Tracking: We use StatCounter to track traffic on our website. StatCounter may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and referring pages.

How We Use Your Information

  • User Registration: We use your username and email address to authenticate your account and communicate with you.
  • Machine Learning Algorithm: The data collected by our machine learning algorithm is used to improve the quality and relevance of the content generated on our website.
  • Traffic Tracking: We use StatCounter's data to analyze website traffic and improve our website's performance.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties.
