Responsible Gambling

Welcome to our Responsible Gambling page. Here, we provide information on how to gamble responsibly and the legal framework governing gambling in Hong Kong.

Hong Kong Gambling Laws

According to the Gambling Ordinance (Cap. 148), all gambling activities are illegal except those authorized by the Government. This includes authorized horse racing, football betting, and the Mark Six lottery. Unauthorized gambling is prohibited and can lead to severe penalties.

Citation: Gambling Ordinance (Cap. 148) of Hong Kong. Retrieved from Hong Kong e-Legislation.

How to Gamble Responsibly

Here are some tips to help you gamble responsibly:

  • Set a budget for gambling and stick to it.
  • Do not chase losses; know when to stop.
  • Take regular breaks and avoid gambling for long periods.
  • Seek help if you feel you are losing control over your gambling.

Support and Resources

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, there are resources available for support:

  • The Hong Kong Jockey Club offers support and counseling services.
  • Non-profit organizations such as Gamblers Anonymous provide support groups and resources.
